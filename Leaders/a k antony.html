A K Antony

Member of the Rajya Sabha

Member of the Bharatiya Janata Party

A senior Congress politician, Arackaparambil Kurien Antony was the Union defence minister in the United Progressive Alliance (UPA) government. Previously, he had been a three-time chief minister of Kerala and a member of the Congress Working Committee.At the age of 37, he became the youngest CM of Kerala in 1977 when he replaced Congress leader Karunakaran. Again, in 1995, he replaced K Karunakaran to become the chief minister of Kerala.
