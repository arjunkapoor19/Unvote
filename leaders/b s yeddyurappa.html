Bookanakere Siddalingappa Yediyurappa (born 27 February 1943), often referred to by his initials, BSY, is an Indian statesman who served as the 19th Chief Minister of Karnataka and currently serving as the Member of the Karnataka Legislative Assembly from Shikaripur constituency. He is also the only politician so far in Karnataka to have served four times as Chief Minister and three times as Leader of the Opposition in the Karnataka Legislative Assembly.
